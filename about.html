About Me

Hey there! I’m Leah, a UX/UI designer who has been creating, researching, and testing products for about a year and a half now. I graduated in 2015 with a BA in psychology and gender studies, and went on to spend several years in various social work positions before finally settling on UX, so I’m a strong believer in using research and data to influence the ways we understand and interact with the people we serve. I believe that the best solutions lie where research and empathy meet.

Outside of work, I enjoy coffee, bookstores, comics, and D&D. My design influence comes from a lot of different places, including interior design, fashion, and even animation.

If you'd like to connect, feel free to reach out and I'll get back to you within two business days
